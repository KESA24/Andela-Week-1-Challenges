Waste Management

Waste generation and reuse is an integral part of human activity. Solid waste can be characterized by their source, original use, or physical and chemical composition. Some types of solid wastes are managed by the waste generator, such as industrial wastes, agricultural wastes, and mining wastes. The wastes that are managed by municipalities are called Municipal Solid Waste (MSW). The terrifying increase in the MSW is a vital indicator of natural resource depletion and the exponential population growth.

Again, technology presents us with numerous ways to address our waste problems through sorting waste effectively, to recycling machines, and waste management systems
